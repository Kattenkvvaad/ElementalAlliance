Getting Started

Want to join the Elemental Alliance? Well, you've come to the right page! Here you will find all the information you'll need to get started in the ARPG!

  1. Make sure you have read the rules and will follow them.
  2. Join our Toyhouse World by submitting a join request. We also have an optional Discord Server for members to join if they'd like.
  3. Submit your first character in the Character Submission thread on the World Forums. Everyone gets two free characters when joining the Elemental Alliance, which can be put in any of the Divisions. After your first two, you'll have to purchase an Adventuring Permit for every new character you want to add.
  4. Once your first character has been registered on the website, you may add them to the Toyhouse World.
That's basically everything you need to do to get started! If you're looking for things to do after joining, here's some suggestions:
  • Submit art or writing or partake in a Quest to get Renown and item rewards.
  • Buy or craft items in the Markets. (every new member starts with 500 Renown, after all)
  • Team up with fellow Elemental Alliance members for a Group Quest! (coming soon!)
