Our Rules

All members of this group should follow these rules. The rules apply in every platform that the Elemental Alliance ARPG is hosted on (such as: this website, discord, and toyhou.se world) unless stated otherwise.

These terms may be subject to change.
Last Update: Jan 21 2025

Behavior and Content Rules

  1. Be kind and respectful towards members and staff of the ARPG alike.
  2. This group may be 18+ but we are fully SFW (Safe For Work). Do not post content of any kind that contains things such as sexual themes or heavy/realistic gore.
  3. Use the discord channels and toyhouse forum-threads for their intended purposes. If a staff member tells you to move the conversation somewhere else, or end the conversation entirely, you are expected to listen.
  4. Swearing is allowed, but keep it minimal.
  5. Do not share any content (images, writing, music, etc.) that was generated using AI.
  6. Keep our community spaces free of in-depth conversations about potentially triggering topics such as real-world politics, religion, or wars.
  7. Please do not vent in our community spaces.

Art and Character Rules

  1. You may only use characters that are yours.
  2. We currently only allow writing and drawn 2D artworks to be submitted. This will likely be expanded upon in the future, though.
  3. Only art made after your character has been added to the Masterlist can be submitted for Renown. The only exception for this is your character's initial Masterlist image.
  4. Every member starts with two (2) free character slots. Additional character slots should be bought in the shop using Renown.
  5. We allow both new and pre-existing characters to be used in our group, just keep in mind that pre-existing characters will have to start as new recruits with low-power skills, just like any other character.
  6. Character designs with gore or body-horror are allowed. Please mark any art with the appropriate warning and spoiler when posting them in the discord server or on toyhouse.
  7. We allow visible nipples in submitted characters/art, but only as artistic nudity. Artworks or characters with nipples that are drawn in a fetishizing or sexually suggestive way will not be accepted into the group.
  8. We do not allow visible genitalia in submitted characters/art.
  9. Any kind of characetrs are allowed as long as they are able to interact with the world. They don't necessarily need to be able to hold a sword, but at minimum they should be able to communicate with other characters and interact with items or their surroundings.
