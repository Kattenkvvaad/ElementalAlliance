Our Rules

All members of this group should follow these rules. The rules apply in every platform that the Elemental Alliance ARPG is hosted on (such as: this website, discord, and toyhou.se world) unless stated otherwise.

These terms may be subject to change.
Last Update: Jan 10 2025

Behavior and Content Rules

  1. Be kind and respectful towards members and staff of the ARPG alike.
  2. This is a SFW (Safe For Work) group. Do not post content of any kind that contains things such as sexual themes or heavy/realistic gore.
  3. Use the discord channels and toyhouse forums/threads for their intended purposes.
  4. When submitting or requesting multiples of the same thing as once, please combine them in one message if possible. (Example: if you submit three artworks, please submit them all at once instead of in three separate messages sent within minutes of one another)
  5. Swearing is allowed, but don't do it every other sentence (or more).
  6. Do not share any content that was generated using AI.
  7. Please do not vent in our discord server.

Art and Character Rules

  1. We currently only allow writing and drawn 2D artworks to be submitted. This is likely to be expanded upon in the future, though.
  2. Only art made after your character has been added to the Masterlist can be submitted for Renown. The only exception for this is your character's initial Masterlist image.
  3. Every member starts with two (2) free character slots. Additional character slots should be bought in the shop using Renown.
  4. We allow both new and pre-existing characters to be used in our group, just keep in mind that pre-existing characters will have to start as new recruits with low-power skills, just like any other character.
  5. Character designs with gore or body-horror are allowed. Please mark any art with the appropriate warning and spoiler when posting them in the discord server or on toyhouse.
  6. Artistic nudity is not allowed in this group. Please only submit art that depicts your characters with "doll nudity", meaning no visible nipples or genitalia.
